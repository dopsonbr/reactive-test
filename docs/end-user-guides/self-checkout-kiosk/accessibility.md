# Accessibility

The self-checkout kiosk is designed to be accessible to all customers. Learn about available accommodations and how to use them.

---

## Accessibility Features Overview

Our self-checkout kiosks include:

- **Screen reader support** for visually impaired customers
- **High contrast mode** for better visibility
- **Large text options** for easier reading
- **Audio guidance** with headphone jack
- **Adjustable screen height** (select kiosks)
- **Wheelchair accessible design**
- **Extended timeout options**

![Accessibility Features](images/accessibility-overview-wireframe.png)
*Accessibility options are available on all kiosks*

---

## Visual Accommodations

### High Contrast Mode

Enables a high-contrast color scheme for improved visibility.

**To enable:**
1. Look for the **accessibility icon** (♿) in the header
2. Tap to open accessibility options
3. Select **"High Contrast"**
4. Screen colors change immediately

![High Contrast Mode](images/high-contrast-wireframe.png)
*High contrast mode improves text visibility*

**High contrast features:**
- White text on dark background
- Bright, distinct button colors
- Enhanced border visibility
- Reduced visual complexity

### Large Text Mode

Increases font size throughout the interface.

**To enable:**
1. Tap the **accessibility icon** (♿)
2. Select **"Large Text"**
3. Choose text size: Large, Extra Large, or Maximum

![Large Text Mode](images/large-text-wireframe.png)
*Large text mode increases readability*

**Text size options:**

| Size | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Standard | Default text size |
| Large | 125% of standard |
| Extra Large | 150% of standard |
| Maximum | 200% of standard |

### Screen Magnification

Zoom in on specific areas of the screen.

**To use:**
1. Enable in accessibility options
2. Double-tap with two fingers to zoom
3. Drag with two fingers to pan
4. Double-tap again to return to normal

## Audio Accommodations

### Screen Reader

Audio narration of all screen content for blind or low-vision customers.

**To enable:**
1. Plug headphones into the **audio jack** (located on the side of the kiosk)
2. Screen reader activates automatically
3. Adjust volume using on-screen controls

**Or without headphones:**
1. Tap accessibility icon
2. Enable "Audio Guidance"
3. Audio plays through kiosk speaker (may not be available in all stores)

![Headphone Jack Location](images/headphone-jack-wireframe.png)
*The headphone jack is located on the right side of the kiosk*

### Screen Reader Navigation

| Action | Gesture |
|--------|---------|
| Hear current item | Single tap |
| Select/activate | Double tap |
| Move to next item | Swipe right |
| Move to previous item | Swipe left |
| Scroll down | Two-finger swipe up |
| Scroll up | Two-finger swipe down |
| Go back | Two-finger swipe left |

### Audio Feedback

The kiosk provides audio cues:

| Sound | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| Beep (high) | Item scanned successfully |
| Beep (low) | Error or item not found |
| Chime | Transaction complete |
| Alert tone | Attention needed |

### Volume Control

Adjust audio volume:
1. Tap accessibility icon
2. Use volume slider
3. Or use physical volume buttons on headphone jack panel

## Physical Accessibility

### Wheelchair Access

All self-checkout kiosks are designed for wheelchair users:

- **Clear floor space** in front of each kiosk
- **Lowered screen angle** for seated viewing
- **Reachable card reader** and scanner
- **Receipt slot** at accessible height

![Wheelchair Accessible Design](images/wheelchair-access-wireframe.png)
*Kiosks are designed for wheelchair access*

### Adjustable Height Kiosks

Select locations have height-adjustable kiosks:

1. Look for kiosks marked with **accessibility symbol**
2. Use the **height adjustment button** on the side
3. Screen and reader adjust together
4. Returns to default height after transaction

### Bagging Area

The bagging area is positioned for easy reach:
- Located at comfortable arm height
- Space for shopping bags or baskets
- No lift required to bag items

## Motor Accessibility

### Touch Alternatives

If touch screen is difficult:

**Extended touch duration:**
- Enable in accessibility settings
- Requires longer touch to register
- Prevents accidental taps

**Switch access:**
- Limited kiosks support external switch devices
- Ask associate for switch-accessible kiosk

### Large Touch Targets

All interactive elements are:
- Minimum 44x44 pixels
- Spaced for easy targeting
- Provide visual feedback on touch

### Timeout Extensions

Extended time for completing actions:

1. Tap accessibility icon
2. Select **"Extended Timeouts"**
3. Choose duration: 5, 10, or 15 minutes
4. Inactivity timeout is extended

**Standard timeouts vs extended:**

| Action | Standard | Extended |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Session inactivity | 2 minutes | 5-15 minutes |
| Payment entry | 60 seconds | 3 minutes |
| PIN entry | 30 seconds | 90 seconds |

1. Tap accessibility icon

3. Interface shows only essential buttons

## Requesting Assistance

### Personal Assistance

Store associates are available to help:
- Press **"Help"** button on screen
- Raise your hand in the self-checkout area
- Ask any store employee

Associates can:
- Guide you through the process
- Operate the kiosk on your behalf
- Move to a traditional checkout if preferred

### Dedicated Accessible Kiosk

Some stores have dedicated accessible kiosks:
- Located at end of self-checkout row
- More space for wheelchairs/mobility devices
- All accessibility features enabled by default
- Priority for customers who need them

### Alternative Checkout

If self-checkout doesn't work for you:
- Traditional checkout lanes are always available
- Customer service can process your purchase
- No questions asked - your comfort matters

## Tips for Using Accessible Features

1. **Enable features at start** - Settings persist only for your transaction
2. **Combine features** - Use high contrast AND large text together
3. **Bring headphones** - For private audio guidance
4. **Ask for help** - Associates are trained to assist
5. **Take your time** - Enable extended timeouts if needed
6. **Choose your kiosk** - Look for the accessibility symbol for best experience
