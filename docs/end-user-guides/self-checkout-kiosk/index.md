# Self-Checkout Kiosk User Guide

Welcome to the Self-Checkout Kiosk User Guide. This documentation will help you navigate the self-checkout experience at our stores.

---

## What is Self-Checkout?

The Self-Checkout Kiosk allows you to scan your items, apply discounts, and complete your purchase quickly without waiting in a traditional checkout line. These touch-screen terminals are designed to be intuitive and easy to use.

![Self-Checkout Kiosk Overview](images/kiosk-overview-wireframe.png)
*The self-checkout kiosk terminal*

---

## Quick Start

1. **Tap the screen** to begin your transaction
2. **Scan items** using the barcode scanner or enter SKU manually
3. **Review your cart** and adjust quantities if needed
4. **Link your loyalty account** (optional) to earn points and get discounts
5. **Pay** using your preferred payment method
6. **Take your receipt** and you're done!

## Key Features

### Touch-Optimized Interface
The kiosk is designed for easy touch interaction with large buttons and clear visual feedback.

### Barcode Scanning
Quickly scan product barcodes using the built-in scanner for fast checkout.

### Manual SKU Entry
Can't scan a barcode? Enter the product SKU using the on-screen keypad.

### Loyalty Integration
Link your loyalty account by phone number or email to automatically apply discounts and earn rewards.

### Multiple Payment Options
Pay with credit card, debit card, or mobile payment methods.

## Need Help?

- **At the kiosk**: Press the "Help" button on screen to request assistance
- **Store associate**: Look for staff members wearing "Happy to Help" badges
- **Technical issues**: See our [Troubleshooting Guide](troubleshooting.md)

## Store Information

Self-checkout kiosks are available during regular store hours. Each kiosk supports:

- **Carryout transactions only** - For items you can take with you immediately
- **Standard payment methods** - Credit, debit, and mobile payments
- **Receipt options** - Printed receipt or email receipt
