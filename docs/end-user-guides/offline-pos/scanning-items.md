## Barcode Scanning

### Using the Scanner

The Offline POS connects to a barcode scanner. When configured:

1. **Position the barcode** facing the scanner
2. **Trigger the scan** (automatic or button-press)
3. **Listen for confirmation** - a beep and visual feedback

## Adding Multiple Quantities

### Same Item Multiple Times

**Method 1: Scan repeatedly**
- Scan the barcode once for each item
- Each scan adds +1 to quantity

**Method 2: Add then adjust**
- Scan once to add to cart
- Go to cart to adjust quantity

### Different Items

Continue scanning different products. Each scan adds the new item to the cart.

---

When a product is found, you'll see:

| Field | Description |

| **UPC** | Barcode number |

**Note:** The Offline POS shows basic product information only. Detailed descriptions, images, and inventory levels are not available in offline mode.

## Tips for Efficient Scanning

### Do
- Keep barcodes flat and visible
- Scan at a consistent distance (4-8 inches)
- Wait for confirmation before scanning next item
- Group similar items together

### Don't
- Cover part of the barcode with your hand
- Scan crumpled or damaged barcodes without trying alternatives
- Move the item while scanning
- Ignore "not found" errors - verify and retry

---

## Common Scanning Issues

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Scanner not responding | Check USB connection |
| Barcode won't scan | Try different angles, use manual search |
| Wrong product added | Remove in cart, scan correct item |
| Product not found | Check if product is in offline catalog |
